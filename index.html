Workout Guide v51

Strength + Capacity rotation with back-off sets, Heavy Legs finisher, and Backpacking circuit updates.

Program Rules

Strength Week = Heavy + Back-off
  • Use heavier loads and lower reps.
  • Rest 2–3 minutes on major lifts.
  • After the last heavy set on main compound lifts, reduce weight 15–20% for 1 back-off set of 10–12 reps.
Capacity Week = More Reps + Shorter Rest
  • Use moderate loads and higher reps.
  • Rest 45–75 seconds on most exercises.
  • Backpacking Legs becomes circuit style for hiking endurance.
Workout Strength Week Capacity Week
Upper Body Full 3–4 × 5–8 + back-off on big lifts 3 × 10–12
Heavy Legs 4 × 4–6 + back-off on squat / hinge / thrust 3 × 10–12
Upper Body Microdose 2–3 × 6–8 2–3 × 12–15
Backpacking Legs 3 × 8–10, straight sets 3 rounds circuit, mostly 12–15 reps
